It is based upon the premise that in todaypsilas Internet economy the Web displays a mirror of the real world. Our system uses TeCFlow, a social networking tool developed for the last four years at MIT, to measure popularity and influence of brands and stars by looking at their relative position on the Web. It is based on the simple insight: ldquoYou are who links to yourdquo.
